About Me

I offer integration software expertise informed by my experience spanning many levels of the computing stack including cutting-edge computer architecture research, high-performance HDL design for FPGAs, embedded systems software programming, and seamless sensor integrations. I excel in creating and optimizing software that underpins high-performance, reliable sensor systems for diverse applications. My thorough understanding of both hardware and software systems positions me uniquely to handle complex projects, transforming requirements into tangible, successful outcomes.
